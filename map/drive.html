GOOGLE DRIVE

Google Drive is a cloud storage service developed by Google that allows users to store, share, and access files from any device with an internet connection. It offers 15 GB of free storage space, which is shared across Gmail, Google Photos, and Drive itself. Users can upload various file types, such as documents, photos, videos, and PDFs, and can also create and collaborate on files like Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides in real-time. Google Drive's sharing features make it easy for individuals and teams to collaborate, and it integrates seamlessly with other Google Workspace apps.
